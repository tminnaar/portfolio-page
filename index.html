About Me

Hi! I'm Travis, I'm 28 and have recently started my coding journey in the last few months. I've grown up with computers from a very young age but never thought to build a career off that familiarity until a friend put together and ran a few creative JavaScript activities over Discord earlier this year. Since then, I've been hooked! I then dove straight into Code Academy to learn my html, CSS and JavaScript basics over the summer and got cracking with my projects!

I started this journey to leave the trenches of retail and start a lifelong career in Tech to better the lives of myself and my family. I'm excited by the diversity in work and opportunity in the field and feel like Web Dev is the perfect stepping stone into the industry. I'm drawn the problem-solving nature of coding and the creativity, both visually in design and within the nuts and bolts of JavaScript. Most bootcamps require a full-time commitment and charge a hefty fee. Being a father and the current sole-earner, Founders and Coders allows me a to start my journey risk-free while continuing to support my family in my current job.
